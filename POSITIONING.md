# GEO Positioning

## Working Product Framing

**Lean GEO Audit Toolkit** is a lightweight GEO / AI-search audit toolkit that produces a practical prioritized report and can support a productized audit offer.

It is not trying to be a complete SEO platform.
It is not trying to be an agency operating system.
It is not pretending that a single score can explain search or AI visibility perfectly.

---

## Who It Is For

Primary user:
- operator
- consultant
- founder
- marketer
- technical generalist

Specifically, someone who wants a practical first-pass view of:
- AI-search readiness
- crawler accessibility
- `llms.txt` posture
- citability / answer quality
- basic discoverability and structured-signal gaps

---

## What It Helps With

It helps answer questions like:
- can crawlers and AI systems access the site clearly?
- does the site have obvious `llms.txt` gaps?
- do pages look citation-friendly or thin and vague?
- are there basic discoverability and structure issues worth fixing?
- what are the fastest useful improvements to make first?

---

## What It Does Not Claim

It does **not** claim to:
- predict rankings
- fully model all AI systems
- replace a full SEO stack
- replace a browser-based deep technical audit
- replace strategic content work
- act as a complete GEO agency in a box

It is intentionally a lightweight, report-first toolkit.

---

## Why This Shape Matters

Most repos in this lane are weak in one of two ways:
- too bloated and installer-heavy
- too shallow and hypey

This project is meant to sit in the useful middle:
- small enough to trust
- honest enough to believe
- practical enough to use

---

## How It Supports A Service Offer

The best first offer is a **productized GEO baseline audit**.

This toolkit supports that by:
- producing a first-pass diagnostic report
- surfacing obvious quick wins
- generating a draft `llms.txt` when needed
- creating a cleaner basis for manual review
- making audit output easier to discuss with a buyer

It should be treated as:
1. a proof asset
2. an audit engine for a small service offer
3. possibly a future product wedge

That is a stronger sequence than forcing SaaS framing too early.

## Plain-English Positioning Line

> A lightweight GEO baseline audit for teams that want a credible first-pass view of AI-search readiness without buying into a bloated SEO platform.
